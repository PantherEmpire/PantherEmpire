    Our logistics experts have years of experience in assembling agile networks that keep costs low while making sure the right products get to shelves at exactly the right time.

  • Value add in distribuition

    Value added activities in the warehouse and the open stock yard provide customers with the ability to change, enhance and consolidate product before distributing to retails and end users. We provide a range of value-added services to meet our customers specific needs and promotional activities. We manage and operate both owned fleet and network of partners including last mile delivery. The company has tie ups with third party transporters and have its own fleet of vehicles to manage both FTL and part load shipments. Irrespective of the size of the shipment and its destination, with our extensive tracking system and the information available at our disposal, we choose the most efficient transporter and the size of the shipment to ensure that your shipment reaches its destination in least possible time and least possible cost. Through the use of economies of scale Panther Empire Limited is able to pass on the benefit to its customers of low transport rates from some of the best transportation companies in Kenya.
